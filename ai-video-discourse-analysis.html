Hypotheses

H1 Discourse source is associated with realism/authenticity framing.
H2 Discourse source is associated with human creativity/cultural value framing.
H3 Discourse source is associated with production/control framing.

To capture bidirectional misalignment more directly, a balanced misalignment measure was calculated using the harmonic mean of the official benefit gap and the user concern gap. By this measure, human creativity/cultural framing showed the highest balanced misalignment descriptively. However, bootstrap comparisons suggested caution in interpreting differences between dimensions, as the 95% confidence intervals for both the human-minus-control difference [-0.088, 0.132] and the human-minus-realism difference [-0.068, 0.132] crossed zero.
